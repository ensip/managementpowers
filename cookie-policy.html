About our cookie policy

1. About cookies

Cookies are small data files which are downloaded to your device when you visit our website.

Websites use cookies for many practical reasons, none of which are harmful to the host device nor detrimental to the website visitor. In fact, they are designed to make your online experience more enjoyable.

Some are necessary for the operation of the website. Others are used to compile statistics which enable us to make your browsing experiencing more enjoyable. There are different types of cookies. This site uses technical, tracking and third-party cookies

Useful information on the technical aspects of cookies can be found here: allaboutcookies.org

2. What type of cookies are there?

Cookies can be classified according to their lifetime, their purpose and according to the domain to which they belong.
When talking about lifetime, there are:

  • session cookies, which expire as soon as you close the browser;
  • persistent cookies, which remain on the user’s computer/device for a predefined period of time.

Regarding the domain to which the website belongs, there are:

  • first-party cookies created by the web server of the web page visited by the web user and which have the same domain;
  • third-party cookie stored by domains other than the one the user is visiting at the time. This happens when the web page relies on a file, such as JavaScript, located outside its domain.

These cookies are:

  • necessary cookies: which enable the technical operation of the website and its functionalities;
  • analytical/performance cookies: cookies which collect data about the performance of our website and how you use it. These cookies enable us to ensure our web site operates more efficiently.
  • functional cookies: cookies which enable our website to remember your choices (such as your preferred language) and provide improved or customised features.
